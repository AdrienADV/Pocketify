Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 26, 2026

Pocketify is a mobile companion app for connecting to a Coolify instance. This policy explains what data the app stores and how it is used.

Data stored on your device

Pocketify stores your Coolify API URL, instance URL, API token, and app preferences locally on your device so the app can connect to your chosen Coolify instance. This data is not sold and is not used for advertising.

Data sent to Coolify

When you use the app, Pocketify sends requests directly to the Coolify API endpoint you configure. Those requests may include your API token and the operational data needed to display or manage your Coolify resources. The handling of that data depends on the Coolify instance you use.

Notifications

If you enable push notifications, the operating system may provide a push notification token so notifications can be delivered. Notification permissions are controlled by your device settings.

Clipboard access

Pocketify may read from the clipboard only when you use an action that pastes data, such as pasting an API token.

Analytics and tracking

Pocketify does not include third-party advertising tracking or analytics in this repository.

Deleting your data

You can remove locally stored Pocketify data by signing out in the app or deleting the app from your device. You can also revoke or rotate your Coolify API token from your Coolify dashboard.

Contact

For privacy questions, open an issue in the GitHub repository.
